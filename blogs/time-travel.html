PUBG studio has launched its new version for mobile devices called PUBG: New State. This game is solely developed by Krafton Inclusive and hence does not have any link with Tencent. It was in hype a month ago by the name PUBG 2 but now PUBG has officially dropped its trailer and pre-registration link too. Pre-registered user will get free permanent vehicle skin of this new game. The game has very realistic graphics and have new weapons, drones, and vehicles too.
